Paleo Green Smoothie

INGREDIENTS

dairy free milk 1 cup
kale 2 cups
strawberries 6 pieces
banana 1
hemp seeds 2 teaspoons
chia seeds 1 tablespoon
ice 1 cup
 

STEPS

  1. Place everything in a high speed blender in the order listed in the recipe.
  2. Blend 1 minute or until creamy smooth.
  3. Add more milk or ice cubes for thinner smoothie.
