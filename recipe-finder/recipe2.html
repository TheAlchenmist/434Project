Vegan Mac and Cheese

INGREDIENTS

raw cashews, soaked 3/4 cups
water 1/2 cup
Frank's Hot Sauce 1 teaspoon
white vinegar 41/2 tablespoon
nutritional yeast 1/4 cup
garlic powder 1/4 teaspoon
salt 1/2 teaspoon
your favorite noodle 4 servings
 

STEPS

  1. Place all ingredients (except for noodles) into a blender or food processor and blend until smooth.
  2. Place cheese sauce into a small saucepan and heat over low/medium to your desired temperature.
  3. Pour vegan cheese sauce over your favorite noodles and enjoy!
