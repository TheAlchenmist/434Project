Whole30 Cobb Salad

INGREDIENTS

eggs 3
slices bacon (sugar & nitrate free) 6 slices
chopped romaine lettuce 4 cups
cooked chicken 2 cups (diced)
cherry tomatoes 1 cup (halved)
avocado 1 (diced or sliced)
green onion 3 stalks
extra virgin olive oil ¼ cup
green onion 3 stalks
apple cider vinegar 2 tablespoons
dijon mustard 1 tablespoon
sea salt ¼ teaspoon
ground black pepper &frac18 teaspoon
garlic clove (minced) 1 teaspoon
 

STEPS

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Take out eggs straight from the fridge, then lower the eggs into the water gently, and let it come to a boil again. Once it does, lower the heat, and let the eggs simmer for 11 minutes.
  2. Prepare a large bowl with an ice bath. Once the eggs are done cooking, place them in the ice bath for at least 5 minutes. Peel the eggs under cold running water, then chop them.
  3. Fry bacon slices in a pan over medium heat until even browned and crisp, about 7 minutes. Drain and roughly crumble. Set aside.
  4. Place romaine lettuce on the bottom of a large salad bowl.
  5. Top with chicken, eggs, bacon, cherry tomatoes, avocado, and green onions.
  6. In a separate bowl, combine all ingredients for the dressing and whisk together.
  7. Drizzle over the salad and toss together gently before serving.
